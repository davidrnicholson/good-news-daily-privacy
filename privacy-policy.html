Privacy Policy for Good News Daily

Last updated: [18/12/24]

Information Collection and Use

Good News Daily is committed to protecting your privacy. Our extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

The extension stores only:

  • Daily refresh count (stored locally)
  • Premium status (stored locally)
  • Previously shown article links (stored locally to avoid duplicates)
  • Temporary article history for the back button functionality (stored in memory)

External Services

The extension only connects to specified news sources to fetch RSS feeds:

  • goodnews.eu
  • positive.news
  • goodnewsnetwork.org

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [goodnewsdailyext@gmail.com].
